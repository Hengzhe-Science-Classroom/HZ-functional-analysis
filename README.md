# Functional Analysis · Interactive

**Banach spaces, Hilbert spaces, and operator theory**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 5 · Research Frontier

## What You'll Learn

- Normed spaces and Banach spaces
- Hilbert spaces and orthogonality
- Hahn-Banach, open mapping, closed graph theorems
- Weak and weak* topologies
- Compact operators and spectral theory
- Distributions and Sobolev spaces

## References

- Walter Rudin, *Functional Analysis* (2nd ed., McGraw-Hill)
- John B. Conway, *A Course in Functional Analysis* (2nd ed., GTM 96, Springer)
- Erwin Kreyszig, *Introductory Functional Analysis with Applications* (Wiley)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
